Our Skilled Hands Serve The Best Eatery


Alais Café is a modern-day cafe that serves a variety of your favorite food and beverages from all over the world. Being recognized as the Best Casual Cafe in the year 2019 from Nowhere’s Best Eats, Alais Café is a spot where everyone can relax, have a good time and enjoy good food.

Alais Café serves coffee, from all over the world favorites such as Americano, Espresso, and Vanilla Latte as well as a variety of non-coffee more like Hot Chocolate, or eatery such as our signature menu, Original Crepes. With a vast menu, Alais Coffee is designed to suit everyone's palate and pocket.

Alais Café is the best spot for informal meetings, hang-outs or casual quality time with family. The cozy ambiance, delicious food and refreshing drinks successfully keep customers coming back for more.
